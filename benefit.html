Membership Benefits

WHAT DOES MEMBERSHIP DO FOR YOU?

As the only National Portuguese-American Fraternal Organization, you’ll find a community of Portuguese -Americans who share the same values in culture, language and financial security.

CULTURAL

Shining a spotlight on the Portuguese Culture, Food, Art Language and Sports with like-minded individuals embracing their heritage.

FINANCIAL PRODUCTS

Securing our members future with financial stability through Life Insurance, Annuities, IRA’s and Educational Savings.

PHILANTHROPY

LUSO is known for giving back to our communities from the East to the West Coast, through volunteering and supporting various civic, social outreach and cultural events.

EDUCATIONAL PROGRAMS

To support future generations by nourishing them with various educational programs in conjunction with Luso-American Education Foundation 501c(3) within the Portuguese Culture, Language & Performing Arts. These programs include Cultural Summer Camp, Youth Administration Board, Scholarships & Grants.

DISCOUNTS

As a member of Luso-American Financial, you can get discounts from various vendors through our Perkspot program.
